Privacy Policy

Updated: 2025-09-02

Overview

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Data we collect

  • Account data (name, email, company)
  • Workspace data you upload (jobs, candidates) and ATS sync data (e.g., Greenhouse)
  • Operational logs and telemetry for reliability and security

How we use data

  • Provide and improve our AI recruiting copilot
  • Perform matching, explainability, and workflow actions you request
  • Security, monitoring, and compliance

Open Pool & consent

Sharing to the Open Pool is opt-in and gated by candidate consent. Open Pool profiles are anonymized until consent is granted. You control whether your tenant participates.

Retention & deletion

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the service. You can request deletion of tenant data; we honor legal and contractual obligations.

Security & processors

We use industry-standard security controls. Subprocessors may include hosting, email, logging, and AI model providers. We maintain DPAs as applicable.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.
